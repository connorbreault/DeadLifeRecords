About

Infinito in Torqueo started with Michael Fuentes & Joe Fernandes and was originally called The Eyes of Genocide. After a few lineup changes they settled on their current name (Latin for Eternity in Torture). The band took a short hiatus before signing to Dead Life Records and writing on a new album called "Lost in the Eternity in Torture" featuring 8 tracks.
