About us

BY MUSICIANS. FOR MUSICIANS. Too many times the artists are exploited and used for someone elses benefit. At Dead Life Records we understand the artist because we are artists ourselves. Our mission is to establish a label for both underground and mainstream music to be brought to the masses. At Dead Life we assist our artists with promoting, booking, management, merch making, and digital design. We create and utilize designs for flyers, merch, and video production. We are here to help our bands be more efficient and productive by taking care of the business end of things, allowing our artists to focus on the music! Utilizing every skill our team has to offer to support our bands forward momentum 24/7, this is our promise to you. We push our bands, our bands push us, it's like one big moshpit! This is what makes us much more than a label. All of us here at Dead Life are one big fam-damnly and we are not your typical management team. We will push boundaries.
